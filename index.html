About Us

One Gear Grappling offers Stand-Up and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training for adults and young athletes. Based at Parktown Boys' High School in Johannesburg, the gym is led by former EFC Heavyweight Champion Andrew van Zyl.

Whether you're stepping on the mat for the first time or looking to sharpen your skills, everyone is welcome here.
