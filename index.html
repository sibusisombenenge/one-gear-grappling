About Us

One Gear Grappling was founded on a simple principle: serious training doesn't require a flashy environment. Just mats, a coach who cares, and the willingness to show up.

We train adults of all backgrounds — complete beginners, competitive fighters, and everyone in between. Whether your goal is self-defence, fitness, or competition, we have a place for you on the mat.
